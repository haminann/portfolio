Statistical Modeling  ·  R  ·  Apr 2026

Super Mario Galaxy
Box Office Forecast

Developed a linear regression model in R using 5 day opening gross as the predictor across 11 comparable animated and video game films to forecast the accumulated domestic box office of The Super Mario Galaxy Movie (2026) by May 1, 2026. Cross validated with a log log model, Cook's Distance diagnostics, and an SMB 2023 closest comparable anchor (2.35× multiplier), yielding a final blended prediction of $480M.
